This is a Survey
  • Surveys are anonymous by design. Your identity is never revealed.
  • Surveys do not have a point value and do not appear in the Gradebook.
Notice: This has strict order:
  • You may answer a question only once.
  • Once you move on to the next question, you will not be able to return to any previously answered questions to change your answers.
  • Please review your answers closely prior to clicking on "Next".
  • You may click on "Continue Later" at any time and return to where you left off to finish later.
Notice: This has flexible order:
  • You have the flexibility to move back and forth using the "Next" and "Prev" buttons and the Table of Contents to change your answers.
  • You have the flexibility to use the Table of Contents.
  • You may mark questions to review them again before finishing; these will be tagged for you in the Table of Contents.
  • You may exit at any time by clicking on "Continue later".
Your submission will be completed only when you click on (and confirm) the "Finish" button. If no late submissions are allowed and the due date passes, the system will auto-finish the submission for you, with everything that you have completed up to that point.
Notice:
  • You may exit at any time by clicking on "Continue later".

Notice: This allows multiple tries:
  • The try with the highest score will be used for grading.
Notice: This is timed:
  • The timer will start as soon as you begin.
  • If you begin the Assignment and click "Continue Later", the timer will keep running while you are gone.
